## OS

`debian-bullseye-minimal`

## Post Installing

* **Connect to Wi-Fi**
* **Set resolution**: Preferences > Monitor Settings

## Known issues

* **Unable to connect to Wi-Fi**: Preferences > Advanced Network Configuration, adjust the security level to `WPA & WPA2 Personal`.
